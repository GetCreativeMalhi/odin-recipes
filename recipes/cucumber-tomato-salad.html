Cucumber Tomato Salad

Home

Description

This refreshing cucumber tomato salad made with sliced cucumber, juicy tomatoes, and onion tossed in a sweet and tangy vinaigrette is easy to prepare, has tons of flavor, and is perfect for a warm summer day.

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Vinegar
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Whisk the ingredients for the dressing
  2. Peel and slice the cucumbers
  3. ut the tomatoes into wedges
  4. Slice the onion into rings
  5. Place everything together in a bowl
